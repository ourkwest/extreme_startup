Extreme Startup is almost 15 years old, since it was first created by Robert Chatley and Matt Wynne in 2011. We first ran it in a pub in London, and then at the XP2011 conference in Madrid.

Since then a lot of people have played and run the game, in different ways in different settings. We hope you found it useful, fun, instructive, etc. Now we want to look back and reflect on how people have used the game, and what they and their colleagues have taken from it. We'd really appreciate you taking a few minutes to give us your thoughts and reflections.

We plan to include the aggregated results from this survey in a paper/article/conference presentation. If you would like to see the end result, please add your name and email address below.
